Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a cab with Yara Cab & Taxi?

You can book a cab through our website, by calling us directly at 6355520502, or by sending a message on WhatsApp. We also have a mobile app for easier booking (coming soon).

What are your service areas?

We serve all of Bhavnagar and surrounding areas. We also provide outstation services to major cities in Gujarat including Ahmedabad, Vadodara, Surat, and Rajkot.

Do you provide airport transfer services?

Yes, we provide reliable airport transfer services to and from all major airports in Gujarat. We track flight schedules to ensure timely pickups and drop-offs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI payments, and major credit/debit cards. Corporate accounts with billing facilities are also available.

Are your drivers verified and trained?

Yes, all our drivers undergo thorough background checks and receive regular training in defensive driving, customer service, and road safety.
